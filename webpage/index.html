Predictive Analysis

Using machine learning, we aim to identify patterns and predict potential future hotspots based on past data.

  • Weekend Nights: Higher incident rate predicted around major campus venues.
  • Holidays: Increased likelihood of certain incidents in quieter campus areas.
  • Seasonal Trends: Theft incidents peak at the beginning of each semester.
